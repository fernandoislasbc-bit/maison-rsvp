## The Partnership Pitch (CEO outreach script)

Use this as your email body or opening lines on a call. Personalize the first line with their name and one specific detail about their work — generic copy-paste reads as spam and gets ignored or flagged.

---

**Subject: A digital invitation partnership for [Planner Name]'s couples**

Dear [Name],

I've been following [Studio Name]'s work — [specific detail: e.g., "the Villa Balbiano weddings you've produced" or "your feature in Vogue"] — and the level of craft your studio brings to every detail of a celebration is exactly the kind of partner we built Maison RSVP to work alongside.

My name is Fernando Islas, founder of Maison RSVP. We design bespoke digital invitation experiences — not wedding website templates, but fully original, cinematically animated invitations built once for a single couple, with integrated RSVP and guest management. Every commission we create exists once and is never repeated.

I'm reaching out because the invitation is the first object your couples' guests interact with — before the venue, before the flowers, before anything you produce on the day itself. Right now, most couples are choosing between a generic template site or no digital experience at all. We think your couples deserve better, and we think a partnership between our studios is a natural fit.

**What I'm proposing:**

A referral partnership — when you introduce a couple to Maison RSVP, you receive a commission on the commission, and your couples receive a complimentary upgrade on our Signature collection. Your name appears as a creative collaborator in the final invitation credits, positioning [Studio Name] alongside the other premium elements of the wedding.

There's no obligation and no cost to you. I'd simply like fifteen minutes to show you what we've built — including a full immersive case study we call "The Crossing" — so you can see exactly what your couples would be receiving.

Would you be open to a short call this month?

Warm regards,
Fernando Islas
Founder, Maison RSVP
commissions@maisonrsvp.com
maisonrsvp.ca

*If you'd prefer not to be contacted again, just reply and let me know — I'll respect that immediately.*

### Notes on using this list

- Send personally, one at a time — not a bulk mail merge. A planner who gets a templated mass email will recognize it instantly and it damages your name in a small, relationship-driven industry.
- For the "Contact form only" entries, use the form on their own site rather than guessing an email address.
- Track responses in a simple sheet: Name / Date Contacted / Response / Next Step.
- Lead with London and Lake Como first if your studio's existing client base (Vancouver · London · Lake Como, per your footer) is meant to mirror these markets exactly.
